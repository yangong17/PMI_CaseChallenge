Overview

Welcome to the PMI Summer Case Study Challenge organized by PMI-LA in collaboration with the UCLA Department of Statistics and thePhase 3: Product APhase 3: Product A USC School of Engineering.


This challenge offers participants a hands-on experience working through a project from start to finish, with coaching on PMI best practices. It's designed to strengthen your analytical, decision-making, and presentation skills while providing a valuable opportunity to collaborate and network with peers.
