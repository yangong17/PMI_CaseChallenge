Project Management Lifecycle Experience

Welcome to the PMI-LA Case Study Challenge

This comprehensive case study challenge is organized by PMI-LA in collaboration with the UCLA Department of Statistics and the USC School of Engineering. It provides participants with a hands-on experience working through a complete project management lifecycle, from initiation to closure, guided by PMI best practices and methodologies.

Project Management Lifecycle Overview

1. Initiation

Project Charter and Stakeholder Register

2. Planning

Work Breakdown Structure, Gantt Chart, Cost Baseline, and Risk Analysis

3. Execution

Managing Surprises and Change Requests

4. Control

Monitoring and Updating Project Baselines

5. Closure

Final Presentation and Lessons Learned

Learning Objectives

  • Apply PMI best practices throughout the project lifecycle
  • Develop practical project management skills through hands-on experience
  • Enhance analytical and decision-making capabilities
  • Learn to handle project changes and risks effectively
  • Create professional project deliverables and presentations

Final Deliverable

The final deliverable is a comprehensive PowerPoint presentation summarizing your project management journey, including:

  • Project charter and stakeholder analysis
  • Detailed project plan with WBS and Gantt chart
  • Risk analysis and mitigation strategies
  • Change management approach and lessons learned
  • Final project outcomes and recommendations
  • Personal reflections on project management best practices
