Phase 3: High Tech

Unexpected Challenge

Three months into implementing the high-tech paint booth solution, a critical challenge has emerged. During the final permit approval process, the local fire marshal has mandated an upgrade to the building's sprinkler system to accommodate the new paint booth installation. This unexpected requirement adds an estimated $80,000 to the project budget.


As the project manager, you must now navigate this situation while maintaining stakeholder confidence and project momentum. The challenge involves finding creative solutions to address this budget increase while keeping the project on track.

Tips/Guides

  • Think creatively about funding sources and cost-saving opportunities
  • Consider both immediate solutions and long-term benefits
  • Look for ways to leverage the sprinkler upgrade for additional value
  • Explore potential negotiations with contractors or suppliers
  • Consider if any project elements can be phased or rescheduled
  • Evaluate if other planned upgrades can be combined for efficiency

Final Deliverable

Your final deliverable should showcase your creative problem-solving abilities. There is no single "right" answer - we want to see your innovative approach to this challenge. Consider:

  • Could the cost be offset by future benefits or savings?
  • Are there opportunities to generate additional value from this upgrade?
  • How might this challenge be turned into an opportunity?
  • What unconventional solutions might work in this situation?

Remember, the best solution may not be immediately obvious. Think outside the box and demonstrate how your creative approach maintains the project's value while addressing this unexpected requirement.
