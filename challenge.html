Project Management Case Challenge

A Full-Cycle Project Management Simulation

Welcome to the Project Management Case Challenge

This comprehensive case study challenge was developed by PMI-LA in collaboration with UCLA's Master of Applied Statistics & Data Science Program. It provides participants with hands-on experience working through a complete project management lifecycle, from initiation to closure, guided by PMI best practices and methodologies.

While each scenario includes scaffolding in the form of templates and resources, the challenge is designed to encourage independent problem-solving. You will be expected to conduct your own research, apply critical thinking, and leverage learning tools such as PMI Infinity to develop and deliver your project outputs - mirroring the realities of professional project work.

Project Management Lifecycle Overview

1. Initiation

Project Charter and Stakeholder Register

2. Planning

Work Breakdown Structure, Gantt Chart, Cost Baseline, and Risk Analysis

3. Execution

Managing Surprises and Change Requests

4. Control

Monitoring and Updating Project Baselines

5. Closure

Final Presentation and Lessons Learned

Learning Objectives

  • Apply PMI best practices throughout the project lifecycle
  • Develop practical project management skills through hands-on experience
  • Enhance analytical and decision-making capabilities
  • Learn to handle project changes and risks effectively
  • Create professional project deliverables and presentations
