Case Challenge and Final Presentation Guidelines

Challenge Structure

There are a total of 11 modules in this case challenge, each representing a different scenario you may encounter in a typical project.

After completing all 11 modules, you will consolidate everything you built into a final presentation.

Module Components

Each module will have some of the following components:

  • Overviews of the scenario, outlining the objective(s)
  • Memos from key stakeholders to guide your decision making
  • Templates for you to craft your deliverables
  • Links to relevant resources curated by PMI-LA

Final Presentation Rubric

Your final presentation should include the following sections:

Section Description
1. Executive Summary Overview of the project
2. Problem Statement and Objectives What were your objectives?
3. Scope and Work Breakdown Structure How was the project broken down?
4. Baseline Schedule and Cost What was the planned schedule and cost?
5. Risk and Cost Uncertainty Analysis What were the associated risks and cost uncertainty?
6. Changes Made Were there any unexpected events? How did they affect the project? Did you have to update any projections?
7. Summary How did your results compare with your projections? How did you perform overall?
8. Lessons Learned What were your key takeaways from this challenge? Is there anything you would have done differently?
