Phase 1: Introduction to Case Study

Business Case Introduction

You are the project manager at a forklift manufacturing company. Your main role is to ensure the right decisions are made by balancing stakeholder needs, analyzing risks and opportunities, and guiding the project toward success.


Currently, the paint department is the biggest bottleneck in production, limiting how many forklifts can be completed. This causes other departments to sit idle, wasting time and resources.


Finance wants fast results to improve the bottom line. Operations favors a long-term capital investment to upgrade equipment. Marketing is pushing for new products to boost market presence. Leadership has asked you to lead the project and propose the best solution.
