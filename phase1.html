Phase 1: Introduction to Case Study

Business Case Introduction

You work at a manufacturing company that builds forklifts. Your paint department is the biggest bottleneck that limits how fast you can produce forklifts. This causes a lot of other departments to have idle, unproductive time.

Project Documents

  • Stakeholder register
  • Simulated company financial data
  • Memos:

    • Finance is concerned about budget - thinks priority should be to try and generate revenue as soon as possible
