4.1. Realigning the Budget

Scenario Continuation

Now, your team must update the Cost Baseline Diagram to reflect the financial impact of the change. Whether the adjustment involved new equipment, consulting fees, or alternative solutions, these costs must be integrated into your overall project budget and communicated clearly to stakeholders.

As part of the Control phase, maintaining an accurate and current cost baseline is essential for tracking project performance and ensuring financial accountability. This module focuses on ensuring your revised project budget aligns with the decisions made during change control.

Objective

Update your original Cost Baseline Diagram to reflect the revised project budget. Your updated diagram should:

  • Incorporate any additional costs from the approved change
  • Maintain or revise original budget categories as needed
  • Clearly show the variance from the original baseline
