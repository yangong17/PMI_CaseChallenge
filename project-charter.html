1.1. Laying the Foundation

Overview

You have been selected to lead a high-priority capital project: the installation of a new paint booth at your manufacturing plant. The initiative is part of a broader strategy to increase production capacity.

Your job is to formally initiate the project by developing a Project Charter that defines the scope, objectives, stakeholders, major risks, and key milestones. This document will be used to secure official project sponsorship and alignment among the leadership team.

Objective

Create a Project Charter that:

  • Clearly defines the purpose and expected outcomes
  • Identifies key assumptions and constraints
  • Lists preliminary risks and high-level milestones
  • Names key stakeholders
