High-Tech Paint Booth with Dryer

  • Higher initial investment
  • Significant production increase
  • Longer setup and installation time
  • More complex maintenance
  • Better quality control

Low-Tech Paint Booth

  • Lower initial investment
  • Moderate production increase
  • Faster setup and installation
  • Simpler maintenance
  • Basic quality control

Tips/Guides

  • Calculate ROI, not just upfront cost
  • Consider setup time vs. long-term gains
  • Balance stakeholder priorities carefully
  • Factor in maintenance and training costs
  • Consider impact on other departments
